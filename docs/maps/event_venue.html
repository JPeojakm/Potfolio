event venue

This report aims to assist FLUX, an annual artist event in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, in identifying an optimal venue that balances both the event's budget constraints and the convenience of attendees. Our approach involved using the Average Nearest Neighbor method to analyze the addresses of past attendees to discern clusters of households. A Hot Spot analysis was also conducted to determine the most suitable zip codes based on our findings.
